A Webinar with HOMER Energy and Pike Research

This special edition of the HOMER Energy news is all about one thing - our upcoming Webinar on the Remote Microgrid Market. Take advantage of this opportunity to discuss this developing market with HOMER Energy CEO Peter Lilienthal.

Driven largely by the falling price of solar photovoltaics, the global remote microgrid market will expand from 349 megawatts (MW) of generation capacity in 2011 to over 1.1 gigawatts (GW) by 2017, an amount that at least equals all other microgrid segments combined. The remote microgrid market is expected to grow to $10.2 billion by 2017, and investors and suppliers are starting to take notice.Â  Featuring HOMER CEO Dr. Peter Lilienthal along with Pike senior research analyst Peter Asmus, this webinar will break down the remote microgrid sector by market applications and system requirements, and examine the emerging opportunities in telecom, developing economies, the military, and industrial settings.
