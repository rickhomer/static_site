About HOMER Energy

HOMER Energy (Hybrid Optimization of Multiple Energy Resources) is the world’s leading microgrid modeling software company. HOMER navigates the complexities of building cost effective and reliable microgrids that combine traditionally generated and renewable power, storage, and load management. With over 85,000 users in 193 countries, HOMER Energy is the established global leader for microgrid design optimization, feasibility and market analysis.
