HOMER Explorer

HOMER Explorer is the perfect starting point for new HOMER users. HOMER Explorer uses the full power of the HOMER® Engine with extensive on-page Help and a New Project Wizard that makes it easy to simulate a HOMER microgrid. HOMER Explorer can simulate and optimize simple on-grid and off-grid systems with wind, solar photovoltaics, storage, and backup generation. Other HOMER products can simulate more complex systems.
