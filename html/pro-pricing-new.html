Standard License

Standard licenses are general use commercial licenses that apply to most users. They are available as single-user or concurrent-user licenses:

  • Single-user
  • Concurrent-user

Who should buy?

Select this license option if you are an end user and you want to operate, install, and administer the software yourself, or if you are purchasing a group of licenses for your organization that will be installed as node-locked licenses for individual users.

System requirements:

  • Allows installation of a single copy on a computer running Windows (Vista or higher) for use by a single individual Windows user account
  • Requires a HOMER Energy user account

License terms:

Single-user licenses can be purchased with monthly, annual, or permanent terms.


  • Monthly
  • Annual
  • Permanent

Concurrent licenses, also known as floating licenses, allow a single license to be shared among different users in your organization.

Who should buy?

Select this license if you would like multiple users to access a group of licenses, or if you anticipate needing to transfer licenses.

License delivery

Concurrent licenses can be delivered either as:

  • Cloud-based licenses using HOMER Energy's license server. You distribute the license file to all possible users, and we handle the rest. Requires an active internet connection. Perfect for small businesses that need to share licenses.
  • Network licensing by installing the Reprise License Manager (RLM) on your Windows or Linux server. Perfect for larger organizations with IT support and servers. Requires a minimum purchase.

Pricing is the same for both delivery methods.

Available in both annual and permanent license terms:

  • Annual
  • Permanent

Concurrent-user Annual License

  • Customizable: pay only for the modules you need
  • Includes basic support and all product updates during the license period. You qualify for one named support contact for each seat purchased

Concurrent-user Permanent License

  • Customizable: pay only for the modules you need
  • Flexible: add modules to your license at any time
  • Includes basic support and all product updates during the license period. You qualify for one named support contact for each seat purchased
  • After the first year, continuing basic support and product updates require a Maintenance Package.

HOMER Energy LLC is the world’s leading microgrid modeling software company. The HOMER (Hybrid Optimization of Multiple Energy Resources) microgrid software navigates the complexities of building cost effective and reliable microgrids that combine traditionally generated and renewable power, storage, and load management. With over 120,000 users in 193 countries, HOMER is the established global leader for microgrid design optimization and feasibility, and HOMER Energy is a nexus for the microgrid market.
