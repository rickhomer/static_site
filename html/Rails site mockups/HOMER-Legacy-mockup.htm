HOMER Legacy

HOMER Legacy was created at the National Renewable Energy Laboratory as a tool to find affordable energy solutions for village power. HOMER Energy is the sole distributor of HOMER Legacy, which we continue to make available at no cost. Tens of thousands of people have used HOMER Legacy to design health clinics and village power systems throughout the world.

License term: HOMER Legacy licenses are for a 6-month period and may be renewed indefinitely. HOMER Legacy users are required to fill out a useage survey at the end of each 6-month license period. The terms and conditions of HOMER Legacy are covered by the HOMER software general licensing agreement.

Enterprise licensing: Enterprise licensing is currently available for HOMER products with versions 3 and higher (HOMER Explorer). HOMER Legacy licenses are for a single computer and user.

Support and training: No support or training is available for HOMER Legacy, and HOMER Legacy will never be updated. HOMER Legacy users can receive and provide mutual support via the HOMER Users Group. There is no cost to join or participate.

Voluntary payment program: We have created a voluntary payment program to help defray the costs of keeping HOMER Legacy available. If you wish to help keep HOMER Legacy freely available throughout the world, you can do so by paying for HOMER Legacy in an amount that is comfortable to you.
