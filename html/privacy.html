HOMER Energy Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 1, 2014

This Privacy Policy describes, among other things, the types of information collected about you when you register on the HOMER Energy website at www.HOMERenergy.com (the “Website”); how your information may be used and when it may be disclosed; how you can control the use and disclosure of your information; and how your information is protected.

1. Scope of Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to information collected about you by or through the Website. This Privacy Policy does not cover any information collected through any other web site. Please note that the Website may contain links to other web sites not controlled or created by HOMER Energy. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites, and we recommend that you review the privacy policies of each web site that you visit.

2. Information Collected

Information you provide directly. You are not required to provide information about yourself in order to visit the Website. However, if you register to use the free software on the Website or purchase a subscription to any of the software on the Website, you will be required to provide us certain information about you and your organization, including:

  • Contact and demographic information (such as your name, company / employer, address, email address, and phone number);
  • Your email address;
  • Other Information you voluntarily provide to us.

Combining Information. Please note that all of the information we collect about you may be combined, including to help us tailor our communications to you and to develop content and services.

3. Use of Information

We generally use other information we collect about you to:

  • Improve or enhance our software‘s functionality;
  • Compile and store analytical data;
  • Add to our general knowledge base;
  • Generally enhance our understanding of the market for our products and services;
  • Understand what kind of renewable energy is of interest to you;
  • Understand how you are using our products;
  • Link any project information or data provided to your user account;
  • Complete a transaction that you have requested;
  • Enable you to participate in features such as user replies or comments;
  • Analyze the use of the Website and information about visitors to the Website to understand and improve our content, marketing and service offerings;
  • Prevent potentially prohibited or illegal activities in accordance with the Website‘s Terms of Use; and
  • For any other purposes disclosed to you at the time we collect your information or pursuant to your consent.

4. Disclosure of Information

We may share the information collected from you as follows:

  • With your consent. With your consent, we may share your contact information with third-party business partners. You may opt out of sharing certain information.
  • Aggregation. We may aggregate the data collected and share or sell this data to third parties. Any aggregated information may include information concerning your geographic location, organization size, and similar information, but will not contain any personally identifiable information.
  • Business transfers. We may share your information in connection with a substantial corporate transaction such as a merger, consolidation, asset sale, or in the unlikely event of bankruptcy.
  • Legal requirements. We may disclose information about our users, including contact information, to respond to subpoenas, court orders, legal process, and other law enforcement measures, and to comply with other legal obligations.
  • Protect our Web Sites and users. We may disclose information to protect and defend the legal rights, interests, and safety of HOMER Energy, our employees, agents, and contractors (including enforcing our agreements); to protect the safety and security of users of the Website and members of the public; and as otherwise disclosed in our Terms of Use.

5. Your Choices

You can visit and use the Website without directly providing any personal information; however, as described above, some information may automatically be collected or may be requested so you can take advantage of certain features.

You may also have choices about whether cookies and other similar technologies are placed on your computer or mobile device through your internet browser‘s blocking features.

6. Access

If you choose to share contact information with us, you may request to update, amend, or delete your information by contacting us. We will do our best to comply to the extent reasonably practicable, provided however that certain information may not reasonably be subject to change or removal. Please note that we may need to retain some information about you in order to satisfy our legal and security obligations. For example, some of your information may remain in back-up storage even if you ask us to delete it. In some cases, you may be entitled under local laws to access or object to the processing of information that we hold relating to you.

7. Protection of Information

We are committed to protecting your information. We have adopted commercially reasonable technical, administrative, and physical procedures to help protect your information from loss, misuse, unauthorized access, and alteration. Please note, however, that no data transmission or storage can be guaranteed to be 100% secure and therefore we cannot guarantee the security of any information you transmit to us.

8. Privacy Protections for Children

The Website is intended for adults age 18 or older. Children under the age of 18 are not permitted to use the Website pursuant to our Terms of Use and we do not knowingly collect or store contact information about children under the age of 18.

9. User-Generated Content

Website users may in some instances submit their own content for potential use on the Website. Publication of such user-generated content on the Website is subject to our discretion and compliance with our Terms of Use; provided, however, that we are not obligated to review any such user-generated content. Please remember that any information you disclose becomes public information and exercise caution when deciding whether to disclose your contact, financial, or other personal information in a submission. We cannot prevent such information from being used by others in a manner that may violate this Privacy Policy, the law, or your personal privacy and safety. 

10. Third Party Sites

When you select a link to a third party web site from the Website, you will be leaving Website. Any information that you disclose on the linked third party site will be governed by that site's privacy policy and not by this Privacy Policy.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices and service offerings. If we modify our Privacy Policy, we will update the "Effective Date" at the top of this page and such changes will be effective upon posting.

HOMER Energy LLC is the world’s leading microgrid modeling software company.
