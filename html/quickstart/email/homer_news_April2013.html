HOMER Energy CEO to Attend Clean Energy Ministerial in New Delhi, India

The Clean Energy Ministerial (CEM) is a high-level global forum to promote policies and programs that advance clean energy technology, to share lessons learned and best practices, and to encourage the transition to a global clean energy economy. Initiatives are based on areas of common interest among participating governments and other stakeholders.

Clean Energy Ministerial 4 will be held in New Delhi, India in the coming week. Ministers of Energy and other high government officials from countries around the globe will sit at the table with senior business leaders for frank, in-depth information exchange to advance successful clean energy solutions.

CEM 4 adds microgrids ("Minigrids") to the agenda for the first time. The 23 participating governments account for 80% of global greenhouse gas emissions and 90% of global energy investment.

HOMER Energy CEO Dr. Peter Lilienthal will attend and participate in the minigrid roundtable.

As the Microgrid Market Grows, So Do We

The microgrid market is now forecast to have $40 billion in revenues by 2020. HOMER serves a unique role in this market as the leading platform for microgrid development. Since we sent out the first edition of the HOMER Energy News in February 2010, our goal has been to assist you not only to understand microgrids but also to connect with other people and organizations who wanted to make microgrids and distributed generation happen.

The first big change is that HOMER Energy just moved into new offices - with over twice the space. We are hiring new software developers, new engineers, and new people to support all of them!

Just as the market and HOMER Energy are growing, so changes are coming - to our website, to our newsletter, to our software, and to our training. Our goal, as always, is to bring value to all segments of our customer base - from students and professors using HOMER in their engineering classes and theses to project developers and facility owners creating reliable, affordable distributed generation solutions to technology creators and vendors who are selling to the market.

Newsletter update: Coming in May, HOMER Energy News will be replaced by Microgrid News, with a goal of eventually bringing you twice monthly news about what is happening in the industry. We have teamed up with PrimeCourse Communications to bring you a much more "newsy" newsletter. Yes, we will still have company updates and plenty of HOMER news, HOMER tips, training announcements, opportunities to meet our CEO and other staff, and other HOMER-centered news. But we'll also have much more, including sponsorship and advertising opportunities. (Contact us at advertising@homerenergy.com to get on the pre-release list for the media kit and special rates for the first edition.)

Look for a goodbye/hello email in the coming weeks that introduces the new look and feel and gives you options on what types of email you wish to receive from us.

Software and website update: We are in the final days of getting our newest product, HOMER Explorer, prepared for release. At the same time, our website is getting a new look, with a vastly improved ordering and customer section.

Training and Conferences: Watch for an announcement of a microgrid conference and associated microgrid development workshop very soon. This will be the first in a series of conference/workshop combinations around the globe that bring together serious microgrid players from all sectors, focused on pragmatic and practical issues and solutions.

We also continue to offer private trainings at your facility or ours with an emphasis on your particular training needs.
