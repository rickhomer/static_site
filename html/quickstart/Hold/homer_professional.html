"First Look" Beta Release

HOMER® Professional is HOMER Energy's newest "power user" product, scheduled for release in summer 2014. HOMER Professional is destined to do all that HOMER 2 can, and much more. New features include:

  • Redesigned modern user interface
  • More sophisticated load handling, and more options for load creation, including building loads from measured equipment data
  • Component library for improved management of your preferred equipment
  • Microgrid builder - create and save sets of equipment
  • Maintenance scheduling for wind turbines and generators
  • Multiple PV arrays (not in the First Look version but coming soon!)
  • Wind turbine losses
  • Model less than one year (for expeditions and other temporary situations)
  • Fuel usage data in results
  • Component size and weight in results

We are not yet calling this a full beta release as the product is not yet feature complete. The following HOMER 2 features are missing from the First Look release:

  • Help documents and context-sensitive help
  • Hydrogen
  • Compare simulations
  • File compare
  • Certain graphs

HOMER Professional First Look will not read .hmr files.

Important notice for beta participants!

This beta is intended solely for customer feedback and identification of defects.

When you start the program, you will be requested to enter your email for our website. This email must match the email you used to login to the website.

When you hit the "Calculate" button, your HOMER file will be sent to a secure file repository and stored in a warehouse that will associate the file with your user profile record. The commercial product will have an option to keep your data private (i.e. - it will not send), but this option is not available for the beta.

We will not share your information with any third parties. Our use of these files is limited to: (1) testing our data warehousing system, and (2) examining the types of problems our beta or First Look testers are looking at.


We sincerely hope you'll agree to take a look and provide us with honest feedback regarding the product. You will be asked to accept a special edition of the HOMER End User License Agreement before you install the First Look product. You can preview the license text here.

HOMER Energy LLC is the world’s leading microgrid modeling software company. The HOMER (Hybrid Optimization of Multiple Energy Resources) software navigates the complexities of building cost effective and reliable microgrids that combine traditionally generated and renewable power, storage, and load management. With over 100,000 users in 193 countries, HOMER is the established global leader for microgrid design optimization and feasibility, and HOMER Energy is a nexus for the microgrid market.
