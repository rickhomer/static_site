Managing Partial State of Charge in Microgrids Using Trojan Smart Carbon™ Technology

Join HOMER Energy CEO Dr. Peter Lilienthal, Trojan Senior Applications Engineer Kalyan Jana, and Trojan Market Manager Romina Arcamone for a discussion on how Trojan's Smart Carbon™ Technology manages PSOC in microgrids.

Partial state of charge is a critical issue in the design and management of microgrids. HOMER Energy joins market partner Trojan Battery Company for an educational presentation, followed by a live question and answer session on ways microgrid integrators and operators can develop top-performing systems using HOMER simulations and Trojan industrial batteries with Smart Carbon™ technology.

Join us Tues., Sept. 2, for a live demo on how HOMER users can optimize microgrid designs using HOMER and Trojan Industrial batteries specifically designed to address the impact of Partial State of Charge on cycling batteries in solar microgrid applications.
  • HOMER CEO Dr. Peter Lilienthal will present an overview of the Partial State of Charge problem.
  • Trojan Senior Applications Engineer Kalyan Jana will explain the Trojan Smart Carbon technology.
  • Trojan Market Manager Romina Garcia will present a case study of a solar microgrid at the Spice Village Resort in India, using the HOMER software.
  • There will be a live Question and Answer period, with participants able to send in their questions via chat.
